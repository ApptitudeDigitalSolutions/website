About

 

Our Vision

Simple. Paperless. Recruitment.

Our Mission

We want to digitalise the assessment, development and recruitment world using innovative technology.

Our Technology

We have developed easy to use and interactive Apps which are linked to cloud based storage. Once securely stored we use big data analytics to help improve your recruitment process.

Our Clients

BENEFITS

 

Save Paper

A more environmentally friendly approach. No need for reams of pages for each and every candidate.

Save Time

Less admin. Less writing up reports. Less note taking. Less time spent marking tests.

Save Money

With our digital solutions you will save on printing & postage costs and save on the number of resources needed.

Secure Cloud Storage

Keep your recruitment data stored safely for as long as you need allowing you to review candidates and increasing accountability.

Analytics

Future proof your recruitment by using big data to identify and analyse trends and improve your selection process with AI.

Immersive Experience

Engage with your candidates and assessors more effectively in a more realistic modern working environment.

Our Services

 

Only Tech

We will just provide you with the technology that you need to run your own paperless assessment centre with your own resources. We give you the tablets, Apps and training you need to digitalise your recruitment processes.

Assessment Centre

We will provide you with everything you need to run a paperless assessment centre. We give you as many experienced assessors as you need, tablets and our innovative Apps.

Student Employability

We think the world of recruitment is changing and we want to help students adapt to the paperless approach. We offer a wide range of employability services and want students to use our Apps to gain vital assessment centre experience.
